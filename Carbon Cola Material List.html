Carbon Cola Materials

Safety and Experiment Essentials

Safety Materials

  • Goggles
  • Insulated gloves (for dry ice)
  • Nitrile gloves (for handling chemicals)
  • Tongs

Experiment Materials

  • Digital scale
  • Graduated beakers or containers for water samples
  • Base (soda ash and/or baking soda)
  • Litmus strips for pH testing
  • Dry ice (around 1-2 pounds per gallon of water)
  • Flavorings for water
